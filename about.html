Installation Types

Microwaves can come as hood vents to sit above your stove, built in as cabinets to blend in as well as regular countertop microwaves. Most of our selection are built in, but we dedicated a section to countertop microwaves alone as they are perfect for first apartments.

Heating Types

We are proud to offer both kinds of heating options such as dipolar rotation and ionic reduction. Dipolar rotation build heat from friction, where as ionic conduction is the motion of ionic charge through electrical conductivity whihc results in heat. We offer microwaves with one or both heating types!
